  • Spatial Framework — Flexible data capture via UAV/GNSS, "Walking the Boundary", & Incremental Upgradability.
  • Legal & Institutional — Continuum of tenure, joint ownership (Gender Equity), and shifting surveyors to RSU QA roles.
  • Social Legitimacy — Dispute resolution via geo-flagging and collaborative logs over rigid court systems.

Strategic Alignment

SaaS Evaluation: FIG FFPLA Standards

Webmap.geospatialnetworkug.xyz fully operationalizes the Spatial, Legal, and Institutional pillars using frontier tech (AI, Cloud, 3D).

Spatial Framework
  • Flexible Data Capture: Hybrid basemaps (Esri, OSM) & digitizing support participatory mapping for rapid scaling.
  • Incremental Upgradability: Edge Functions calibrate 30m Open DEMs with RSU CSV data, enabling the accuracy continuum.
  • 3D Cadastre: Dedicated 3D view for condominium plotting addressing urban vertical tenure security.
Legal & Institutional
  • Continuum of Tenure: Unique IDs for "Untitled Land", providing legal visibility to customary rights.
  • Gender Equity: Joint ownership registration supporting SDG 5 to secure women's land rights.
  • Professional Evolution: RSU supervisors oversee quality logs, shifting roles to "system custodians".
The Multiplier Effect
  • Dispute Resolution: Digital geo-flagging builds social legitimacy through transparent agreement.
  • Environmental Stewardship: Dedicated layers link tenure to climate resilience in protected zones.
  • Automated Valuation (AVM): Real-time valuation based on market pins creates a sustainable business model.
Technical Architecture (FAIR): Scalable infrastructure via PostgreSQL, Supabase, and S3 Buckets ensures Findable, Accessible, Interoperable, and Reusable data. An AI Assistant bridges capacity gaps.
Conclusion: Beyond a mapping tool, it's a comprehensive Spatial Data Infrastructure (SDI) fully aligned with the global roadmap for universal land tenure security by 2030.
Security & Compliance

Uganda Data Protection Act 2019

GSPNET is engineered with Privacy by Design, fully complying with the Personal Data Protection Office (PDPO) mandates.

  • Data Minimization — We collect only the minimum PII necessary for professional land registry work.
  • Strict Access Control — Role-Based Access Control (RBAC) ensures sensitive ownership data is only accessible to authorized actors.
  • Explicit Consent — Robust consent flows before data collection, ensuring accountability, fairness, and lawfulness.
